What is Afromation?

Afromation is a pioneering African storytelling studio dedicated to creating cinematic narratives that celebrate the continent's diverse cultures while embracing modern innovation. Founded as a subsidiary of ABION Industries Ltd, we bridge the gap between traditional African storytelling and contemporary media formats.

Our mission is to amplify African voices on the global stage through compelling stories that resonate with audiences worldwide. We produce original content across multiple platforms, including animated series, short films, and digital experiences that showcase the richness of African heritage.

At Afromation, we believe that stories have the power to transform perceptions, build connections, and inspire future generations. Our team of talented writers, animators, and filmmakers work collaboratively to bring authentic African narratives to life with exceptional quality and artistic vision.
